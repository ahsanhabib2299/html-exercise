Rccipe for the Best cinnamon Rolls

ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg youlk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

for the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yest,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuites
  5. Transfer the dough into a large blwl and cover with plastic warp. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After rising, roll into a long thin roll then cut into eight rectangles.
  7. Spread butter on rectangles then roll it into a log shape.
  8. Melt the butter for the filling and cover in sugar and cinnamon.
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a biking tray
  10. Preheat oven to 350°F or 180°C, then place rolls into a greased pan.
